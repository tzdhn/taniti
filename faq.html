Frequently Asked Questions

What currency is used in Taniti?

Taniti uses the US Dollar. Many businesses also accept Euros and Yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and major credit cards are accepted at most businesses.

What are the alcohol laws?

Alcohol may not be served or sold between midnight and 9:00 AM. The drinking age is 18.

Is Taniti safe for tourists?

Violent crime is very rare. As tourism has grown, there are occasional reports of pickpocketing in busy areas. Standard travel precautions are recommended.

What type of power outlets are used?

Power outlets operate at 120 volts — the same as the United States. US-standard plugs work without an adapter.

Is English widely spoken?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English, especially in Taniti City. In rural areas, very little English may be spoken. The hospital has multilingual staff.

Are there medical facilities on the island?

Taniti has one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has multilingual staff. Travel insurance with medical coverage is recommended.

What should I know about national holidays?

Taniti celebrates many national holidays. Many tourist attractions, restaurants, and shops close on these days. Check the holiday calendar before planning specific activities.
