Where to Stay

All lodging on Taniti is strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Taniti's only four-star resort. Spa, multiple pools, private beach access.

Location: Yellow Leaf Bay  |  Cost: $$$$

Taniti City Hotel ★★★

Mid-range hotel in the heart of Taniti City. Walking distance to beaches and dining.

Family-owned bed and breakfast. Homemade breakfasts and garden views.
