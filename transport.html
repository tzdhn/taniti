Getting To & Around Taniti

Arriving on Taniti

  • By Air: Taniti is served by a small airport accommodating small jets and propeller planes. Airport expansion to welcome larger jets is underway.
  • By Cruise Ship: A small cruise ship docks at Yellow Leaf Bay once per week for one night.

Getting Around the Island

Taniti City and Merriton Landing are very walkable, many visitors never need a vehicle.
  • Public Bus: Serves Taniti City daily, 5 AM–11 PM. Private buses cover the rest of the island.
  • Taxi: Available throughout Taniti City.
  • Rental Car: Available from the local agency near the airport. Best for exploring rural areas.
  • Bike Rental: Bikes and helmets available from several vendors. Helmets are required by law.
  • Walking: Taniti City is flat and very walkable. Merriton Landing is also easy to explore on foot.
  • Hotel Shuttle: Many hotels offer complimentary airport and island shuttles.
